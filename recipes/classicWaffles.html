Classic Waffles

Classic Waffles:

Turns out lovely and crispy. Perfect for any day of the week!

Classic waffles are a beloved breakfast staple that have been enjoyed for generations. They are made by pouring a batter made from eggs, flour, sugar, and milk onto a hot waffle iron, then cooking until golden brown and crispy. The result is a fluffy, tender waffle with a crisp exterior that is perfect for topping with butter, syrup, fruit, or whipped cream. Classic waffles are a simple yet satisfying breakfast option, and are a great way to start the day with a warm and delicious meal. Whether you enjoy them plain or topped with your favorite sweet or savory ingredients, classic waffles are sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ½ cups warm milk
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.

  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.

  4. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
